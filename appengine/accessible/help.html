Accessible Blockly Music Game: Help

Accessible Blockly is a version of Blockly designed for use with screen readers. It consists of two sections: a toolbox and a workspace.

The workspace can contain various groups of blocks; use Tab or Shift-Tab to navigate between these groups. On individual blocks, you can press up or down to go to the next or previous block. You can also move right to access the block's submenu, which allows you to modify the block's inputs, or cut, copy, move or delete the block. It is also possible to mark the destination spot for a block and then move the block to the marked spot.

To complete a level, create a single group of blocks in the workspace that satisfies the instructions. When this group of blocks is run as a program, you'll be able to listen to the music you've made, and adjust the program as needed.
